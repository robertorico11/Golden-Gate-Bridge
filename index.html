Welcome to the Home Page of Golden Gate Bridge

Each year the Golden Gate Bridge attracts more than 10 million visitors to take in its tremendous 746-foot tall towers, sweeping main cables, signature International Orange color and Art Deco styling. It is a sensory experience featuring color, light and sound. The bridge that is in Between San Francisco Peninsula and Marin Headlands is called Golden Gate Bridge because the bridge is situated over the strait that is called Golden Gate, so with this way we can say that San Francisco is the city which city is known as the city of golden gate.
